Wave to Earth -Wave to Earth is a South Korean indie band known for their soothing blend of jazz, indie pop, and alternative music.

With their minimalist yet intricate arrangements, Wave to Earth crafts music that feels both intimate and expansive, resonating deeply with listeners.

Their artistic approach and unique sound have garnered a dedicated following, establishing them as a distinctive presence in the indie music scene.
