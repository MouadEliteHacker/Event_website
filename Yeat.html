Yeat - Yeat, an American rapper and songwriter born Noah Olivier Smith, has rapidly gained recognition for his distinctive blend of trap and experimental hip-hop.

Known for his unique vocal style and energetic performances, Yeat incorporates a mix of melodic hooks and hard-hitting beats that captivate his audience.

Emerging from the underground scene, his music often features themes of hedonism and personal struggle, resonating with a diverse and growing fanbase.

With a rapidly expanding discography and collaborations with notable artists, Yeat continues to carve out a unique space in the contemporary rap landscape.
